## 💡 Pro Tips

### 1. Data Organization
```
Put your documents here:
data/raw/china/policies/        → China PDFs
data/raw/china/market_data/     → China Excel
data/raw/india/regulations/     → India PDFs
data/raw/india/market_data/     → India Excel
```

### 2. Query Examples
```
"What EV incentives does China offer?"
"Forecast India's auto sales for 2027"
"Compare China and India policies"
"What's the projected EV adoption in India?"
"Recommend strategy for Indian OEMs"
```

### 3. Forecasting Tips
```
- Use at least 24 months of historical data
- Include both sales volume AND percentages
- Upload seasonal data (monthly preferred)
- Test with different forecast periods (12, 24, 36 months)
```

### 4. Policy Analysis Tips
```
- Upload policy PDFs with clear keywords
- Include both China and India documents
- Run extraction before comparison
- Review alignment scores carefully
```
